Smart pre-check

Recommended first

Before you dive into detailed guides, confirm these basics:

  • Power cables and switches are firmly connected.
  • Your internet/router lights look normal (no blinking red).
  • You’ve tried turning the device off and on again.

1‑minute universal quick fix

  1. Restart the affected device (phone, laptop, router, etc.).
  2. Wait 30 seconds before turning it back on.
  3. Check if the issue persists in a different app or website.

Instant answers

FAQ
  • Why is my Wi‑Fi connected but I have no internet? ▾
    Restart your router and modem, then test a different device. If only one device is affected, forget and re‑add the Wi‑Fi network on that device.
  • My laptop is very slow. What can I try? ▾
    Close unused apps and browser tabs, restart the device, and ensure you have at least 10–15% free disk space. If it’s still slow, run a malware scan.
  • An app keeps crashing. How do I fix it? ▾
    Update the app, clear its cache (or temporary files), and restart your device. If the issue persists, reinstall the app and check for OS updates.
  • Is remote access support safe? ▾
    Only allow remote access from trusted technicians, and only for the duration of the session. We never store your passwords or files.

Guided quick fixes

Choose the issue that best matches what you’re seeing. Each card gives you a short, safe sequence of steps you can try immediately.

Network • Home / Office

Wi‑Fi connected, but no internet

Use this when your device shows full Wi‑Fi bars but websites or apps won’t load.

Step 1 Restart your router and modem (unplug for 30 seconds).
Step 2 Test another device on the same network.
Step 3 On the affected device, “Forget” the Wi‑Fi network and reconnect.
⏱ 5–8 minutes Difficulty: Easy
Performance • Windows / macOS

Computer is slow or freezing

Ideal when everything feels sluggish, apps take ages to open, or your cursor keeps spinning.

Step 1 Close unused apps and browser tabs.
Step 2 Restart the computer and install pending system updates.
Step 3 Check free disk space and remove large unused files.
⏱ 10–15 minutes Difficulty: Medium
Apps • Desktop / Mobile

App keeps crashing or won’t open

Use this when a specific app closes by itself, freezes, or refuses to start.

Step 1 Update the app from the official store or vendor site.
Step 2 Clear the app cache or temporary files.
Step 3 Reinstall the app and restart your device.
⏱ 6–12 minutes Difficulty: Easy
Devices • Printer

Printer shows “offline” or won’t print

Perfect when your printer is powered on but your computer says it’s offline.

Step 1 Power‑cycle the printer and check for error lights.
Step 2 Ensure the printer and computer are on the same Wi‑Fi network.
Step 3 Re‑add the printer from your system’s printer settings.
⏱ 8–12 minutes Difficulty: Medium
